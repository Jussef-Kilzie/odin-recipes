Lasagna

Description

Lasagna- an old time classic!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Bell peppers
  • Onions
  • Tomato Sauce, tomato paste, crushed tomatoes
  • Ricotta, shredded mozzarella, parmesan
  • Meat sauce
  • Noodles

Steps

  1. Start by making the sauce with ground beef, bell peppers, onions, and a combo of tomato sauce, tomato paste, and crushed tomatoes.
  2. Let this simmer while you boil the noodles and get the cheeses ready.
  3. From there, it's just an assembly job. A cup of meat sauce, a layer of noodles, more sauce, followed by a layer of cheese. Repeat until you have three layers and have used up all the ingredients.
  4. Bake until bubbly and you're ready to eat.
